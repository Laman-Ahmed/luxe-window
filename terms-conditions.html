04

Maintenance & Care

Please read this section carefully — proper care during the curing period is essential to the longevity of your installation.

Post-Installation Care

After installation, it is common to notice water spots or bubbles on the window film. These are normal and will naturally dissipate as the film cures. The curing period can last up to 45 days. During this time, it is essential to avoid cleaning the windows to allow proper curing.

Inspection After Curing Period

If water spots or bubbles remain after the curing period, we will be happy to come back and inspect the film to assess the situation and take any necessary action.

Cleaning Instructions

Once the curing period is complete, you may clean the window film using a non-abrasive cloth and a mild, non-ammonia-based cleaner. This will help maintain the appearance and integrity of the film.

Avoid Harmful Cleaning Materials

To avoid damage, do not use solvents, rough sponges, or abrasive cleaners, as these can scratch or degrade the film. Always use gentle cleaning methods to ensure the longevity of the window film.
