What Is Solar Control Film?

The Smart Way to
Control Your Glass

Solar control window film is a thin, professionally applied film that works with your existing glass to regulate temperature, reduce glare, and block harmful UV radiation. Unlike replacing your windows entirely, solar film delivers immediate, measurable improvements at a fraction of the cost.

Applied to the inside surface of your glass, solar films work by reflecting and absorbing a percentage of the sun's energy before it enters your space. The result is a cooler, more comfortable environment in summer — while still allowing plenty of natural light through.

Available in a range of finishes including reflective, neutral, and tinted options, solar control film can be tailored to suit any property — whether you want a subtle, barely-visible finish or a bold, reflective appearance.

Every installation is carried out by our certified team and backed by a manufacturer warranty of up to 10+ years.

Key Benefits

How Solar Control Film
Benefits Your Property

One installation. Immediate results. Here's what solar control film delivers for your home or business — from day one.

Solar Heat Rejection

Reduce the amount of solar heat entering your space by up to 79%, keeping rooms significantly cooler in summer without blocking your view or natural light. Ideal for south and west-facing windows that get the most sun exposure.

Glare Reduction

Significantly reduce harsh glare on screens and surfaces, improving comfort and productivity in offices, lounges, and workspaces — without darkening the room.

99% UV Protection

Block up to 99% of harmful UV rays to protect your floors, furniture, fabrics, and artwork from fading and sun damage — extending the life of your interiors.

Lower Energy Bills

By reducing solar heat gain in summer, you rely less on air conditioning and cooling systems — leading to measurable reductions in your energy usage and bills. Some Low-E films also help retain heat in winter, making the film effective all year round.

Daytime Privacy

Reflective solar films provide daytime privacy by preventing outside views in, while still allowing you to see out clearly — ideal for ground floor or street-facing windows.

Enhanced Appearance

Solar films give your windows a clean, modern finish. Reflective and neutral options elevate the exterior look of your property, adding a polished, premium aesthetic.
