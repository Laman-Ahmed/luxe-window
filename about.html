About Us

Built on Professionalism
& Quality

Luxe Window Film is a premium window film installation company based in Essex, serving residential and commercial properties across England. We specialise in the professional supply and installation of high-performance window films — delivering real, lasting improvements to every property we work on.

Our team is fully trained, certified, and experienced across all film types — from solar control and privacy films to security, frosted, and conservatory solutions. Every installation is carried out to the highest standard, with care and attention given to every detail from start to finish.

We operate across 16 counties throughout England, working with homeowners, businesses, landlords, and commercial fit-out teams. Whether you need a single window treated or a full commercial building fitted, we bring the same level of professionalism and commitment to every job.

At Luxe Window Film, we believe in doing the job right — every time. That's why we only use premium-grade films backed by manufacturer warranties of up to 10+ years, and why our customers consistently rate us 5 stars on Google.
